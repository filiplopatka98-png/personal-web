## Problem

The existing shop ran on an outdated WooCommerce theme, took 6+ seconds to load on mobile, and Google Search Console permanently flagged "Page Experience: Poor." Conversion rate 1.2% at 8,000 monthly visits. The client couldn't change content without involving a developer.

## Solution

Custom theme built from scratch, composable Gutenberg blocks for product pages, lazy-loaded galleries, image optimization via ShortPixel, inlined critical CSS. For admins: simplified block library, ready-made layouts for categories and campaigns.

Performance:
- LCP 4.8s → 1.4s
- CLS 0.21 → 0.03
- JS payload -73%

## Result

After 3 months:
- Conversion 1.7% (+40%)
- Bounce rate -18%
- Client has shipped 4 campaign landing pages on her own
