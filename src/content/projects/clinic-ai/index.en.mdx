## Problem

The clinic's reception was flooded with 200+ daily calls about repeating questions: procedure prices, appointment availability, pre-op prep. Reception couldn't keep up; patient communication quality suffered. The client wanted a pilot AI assistant on the site before investing in a full call center.

## Solution

Next.js chat widget integrated into the existing WordPress, GPT-4o + custom RAG over an internal database (~400 procedures, pricing, FAQ). Streaming responses, fallback to live agent after 3 unsuccessful attempts, full audit log for compliance. Rate limiting per IP and per session.

Costs:
- ~12,000 queries/month
- $84/month OpenAI cost
- 35% of inquiries resolved without human intervention

## Result

After 6 weeks in production:
- Reception calls -22%
- Average patient response time: from 4 hours → 8 seconds (for common questions)
- Client decided not to expand call-center headcount
- NPS for digital channel: 72 (before: not measured)
