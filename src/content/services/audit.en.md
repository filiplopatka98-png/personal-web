I'll examine your existing site through 4 lenses: performance, SEO, accessibility, security. You'll get a written report with a prioritized list — critical first (site is broken / unusable), then important (lowers conversion / SEO score), finally nice-to-have (small details).

Performance: Lighthouse on 5 representative pages, WebPageTest on 3G/4G profiles, Core Web Vitals from Search Console. I identify render-blocking resources, unoptimized images, third-party script bloat, layout shift culprits.

SEO: hreflang if you're multilingual, schema.org markup, internal linking graph, canonical tags, sitemap completeness, meta description coverage. I often find 10-20 pages without a meta description — minor, but -10% CTR from Google.

Accessibility: axe automated audit + manual keyboard testing (5 most important flows), color contrast, ARIA roles, focus order. EU laws are pushing accessibility into compliance — better to prepare now than pay fines later.

Reports are delivered within 5-7 business days. After delivery, a 30-min call where we go through what to fix and in what order.
